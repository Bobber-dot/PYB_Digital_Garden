### Three Key Factors of Material Characterization 材料表征的三要素
- Motivating source: what kind of ==beams== are employed; 
- Interaction mechanism: how the beam ==interacts== with materials and what kind of signals will be generated; 
- Signal acceptor and analysis：what kind of methods are used to ==accumulate the signals==.

### Structure Characterization 结构的表征分为以下部分

Elemental structure (Composition) 组成结构
1. Integral composition 
2. Localized composition 
3. Compositions of surface and boundaries 

Phase structure 相结构
1. Polycrystalline phase constituent 
2. Chemical constituent: valence bond, copolymer, etc.

Microstructural analysis 微观结构分析
1. Morphology in all scales and areas 
2. Phase distributions and orientation relationship in polycrystalline materials

Crystal structural analysis 晶体结构分析
1. [[结构表征/Crystal Structural Characterization\|Crystal structures in all scales and areas]] 各种尺度下的晶体结构
2. Defects of phases 晶体的缺陷

Chemical structural analysis 化学结构分析
- Molecular structure and chemical bonds
